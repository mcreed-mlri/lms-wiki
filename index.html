LACE Platform

Building the future of legal aid learning in Massachusetts.

LACE is MLRI's practice-centered learning initiative for strengthening legal aid through accessible training, shared resources, and long-term learning infrastructure for advocates across the Commonwealth.

Platform Home

Project North Star

Purpose

Strengthening legal aid requires strengthening learning.

MLRI is developing LACE to expand access to high-quality, evidence-informed training for the Massachusetts legal aid community. The platform should make practical legal knowledge, skills-based training, curated resources, and practice tools easier to find, use, revisit, and share across organizations.

North Star
NeedLACE Response
Training can be fragmented or hard to access.Create an easily navigable online learning platform with shared legal aid training and resources.
Advocates need learning that fits day-to-day practice.Prioritize practical, skills-based, multimedia, self-paced learning grounded in adult learning principles.
Organizations need reusable knowledge infrastructure.Build long-term access to legal knowledge, curated resources, and ready-to-use practice tools.
The pilot needs a focused starting point.Begin with Legal Foundations, Pre-Trial Skills, and Trial Skills while the infrastructure matures.
