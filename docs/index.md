## Open Questions — Needs Decisions

These are blocking or important enough to track. Update this list as decisions get made (and record them in the [Decision Log](decisions/decision-log.md)).

- [ ] **Versioning threshold** — When do we archive a course and create a new offering vs. updating in place? Define the line between "minor" and "major."
- [ ] **Learner update notices** — If enrolled learners need to know about a significant content change, how do we reach them? (Email may not be reliable enough for legal updates.)
- [ ] **Sections vs. new Offerings** — For different cohorts or scheduled runs, do we use Brightspace Sections inside one Offering, or create separate Offerings?
- [ ] **Discover page vs. custom catalog** — Are we using Brightspace's built-in Discover page, our own custom frontend catalog, or both?
